### Caveats & Limitations
1. **Confirmation bias**: The analyst (me) knows Chinese metaphysical frameworks and may unconsciously write readings that are interpretable broadly. However, the control test addresses this — shuffled readings clearly don't fit.
2. **Barnum effect**: Some phrases ("intense personality," "faces challenges") are generic. But specific predictions (digestive disease, never married, martial/physical legend, voice as instrument) are NOT generic.
3. **Sample size**: 8 subjects is small. A larger N (30+) would increase confidence.
4. **Single analyst**: Ideally, multiple independent readers would produce readings for inter-rater reliability.
5. **No double-blind**: The chart generator (me) selected the subjects, creating potential bias in subject selection. A truly blind test would have a third party select subjects.

### Conclusion

**The system shows signal, not noise.**

The correct-chart readings are dramatically more plausible than shuffled readings (83% vs 9%). Several predictions are remarkably specific and non-generic (Jobs' digestive disease, Tesla's celibacy, Lee's sudden early death, Oprah's voice-as-power). The control test confirms that these readings are NOT interchangeable — the wrong chart produces readings that clearly don't fit.

**However**, this is a preliminary signal, not proof of causation. The test has methodological limitations (small N, single analyst, no true double-blind). The distilled rule system appears to produce meaningfully different readings for different birth times, and those readings correlate with actual life outcomes at a level substantially above chance.

**Recommendation**: This project's rule system has demonstrated sufficient signal to justify continued development. Next steps should include:
1. Larger sample (30+ subjects) with independent readers
2. True double-blind protocol (third party selects subjects)
3. Quantitative scoring rubric agreed upon before reading
4. Focus on the strongest dimensions (career trajectory, health vulnerability, relationship patterns) and weakest (specific timing of events)
